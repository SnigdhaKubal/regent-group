About Us
Backed by two decades of automotive expertise, we have become a trusted dealership for premium brands in the Mumbai and Thane regions. Our journey started in the year 2014 with Regent Honda in Thane, followed by swift expansion into Kalyan in 2015. In 2019, we proudly launched MG dealerships in Thane and Navi Mumbai, with cutting-edge facilities added in 2021. Recently, we have expanded further with Toyota and Škoda dealerships in 2023 and 2024. Our advanced facilities offer comprehensive automotive services, all built on trust, innovation, and long-lasting partnerships.
